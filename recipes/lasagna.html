Lasagna


Recipe for a mouth watering lasagna

The recipe for this lasagna is simple and easy to grasp making it easy to learn

The ingredients for this lasagna are really basic making it easy to cook anywhere and at anytime.

Ingredients

  • parmesan
  • ground beef
  • noodles
  • fresh basil
  • garlic
  • cottage cheese
  • eggs

Steps

  1. Firstly, add breakfast sausage coupled with ground beef and garlic to a pot
  2. then you'll put in whole tomatoes, a couple of tomatoe pastes, dried parsely and salt
  3. stir till it looks good
  4. next is to cook the noodles
  5. then in a bowl , break in a couple of eggs about 2 or 3 plus cottage cheese, parmesan ,dried parsely and salt. Then stir
  6. inside a big bowl , do some layering at the bottom followed by the mix from the step above then some cheese on top then add the mix from step 3
  7. repeat the layering process , then more cheese on top with that mix from step 3
  8. repeat one more time with what you have left of step 3 mix (meat sauce) then add parmesan
  9. then finally put in the over for some time
  10. Serve when done
