Pasta


Recipe for a mouth watering pasta

The recipe for this pasta is simple and easy to grasp making it easy to learn

The ingredients for this pasta are really basic making it easy to cook anywhere and at anytime.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Olive oil
  • Sea salt

Steps

  1. 1. Firstly, make a nest in the flour
  2. 2. Break the eggs in the flour
  3. 3. Incorporate the flour
  4. 4. Knead for 5-10 min
  5. 5. If it's too dry , sprinkle water
  6. 6. If it's too dry, add dust flour
  7. 7. Shape into a ball
  8. 8. Wrap and rest for 30 min
  9. 9. Cut into 4 pieces then flatten into a disk
  10. 10. USING LEVEL 1 ,roll through pasta attachment
  11. 11. Fold the ends of that pasta towards the center, then fold in half
  12. 12. USING LEVEL 2 & 3 ,roll 3 times
  13. 13. USING LEVEL 4, 5, & 6 ,roll once
  14. 14. Run through pasta attachment
  15. 15. Cook fresh pasta in boiling water for 1-2 min
