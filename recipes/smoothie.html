Smoothie


Recipe for a mouth watering smoothie

The recipe for this smoothie is simple and easy to grasp making it easy to learn

The ingredients for this smoothie are really basic making it easy to cook anywhere and at anytime.

ingredients

  • 1 cup frozen mixed berries
  • 1 frozen ripe banana
  • 1/2 cup low-fat vanilla yogurt
  • 1/4 cup orange juice
  • 1 teaspoon honey (optional)

Step

  • Combine all ingredients together in a blender and puree until smooth
